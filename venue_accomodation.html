Castle

The middle floor of the Castle consists of various magnificent State Rooms which appear much as they did at the time of Karl XV. All rooms are located on the upper floor and are large with expansive views over the gardens.

No elevator access is available to Castle rooms

                             

Klostergården


Room Type Cost 4-10 Aug 2026
Single 11100 SEK/person
Double (non-Seperable) 8100 SEK/person

Klostergården (the Old Monastery) is situated in the court yard, close to the Castle.

The rooms are quite close to the original kitchen and are either on the ground floor or the first floor, with views to the inner courtyard or to surrounding the gardens and pastures.

Vapensmedens Hus


Room Type Cost 4-10 Aug 2026
Twin Share 4200 SEK/person

Originally used to forge weapons, this small house stands close to Karl XV's tavern and Lake Oppmanna. The rooms are charming and original with iron-poster beds as an interesting detail. Views include the gardens and the lake. However, due to its small size, the bathroom facilities are shared. The rooms are medium sized.
