What is the MidSEC Conference?

The 2026 MidSEC Conference is the inaugural in-person meeting of the community.

The meeting will be organized with a variety of events from traditional plenary and research presentations to lightning talks, posters, discussions, technology showcase, strategy sessions, vendors and plenty of opportunities to talk to others. Our hope is to convene a diverse group of scientists and engineers at all stages of their careers.
