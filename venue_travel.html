OUR RECCOMMENDATION


Fly to Copenhagen--> Train to Kristianstad--> Shuttle to Venue


The closest large town to Bäckaskog Castle is Kristianstad (see map).

A bus will be provided to transport conference attendees from Kristianstad Central Railway Station to the venue. This bus will operate on Tuesday August 4, every hour between 14:45 and 19:45. Each bus can accommodate 50 people, passengers arriving in excess of that may be asked to wait for the next shuttle.

The bus will operate again on Monday August 10, to return attendees to Kristianstad Station for departure.




Getting to Kristianstad



TRAIN

A train service to Kristianstad Central departs from Copenhagen Airport. The direct trip (Track 1) departs hourly, on the hour, takes 1h 34m and costs 300 SEK one way.
